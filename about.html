Our Story

Govt. Girls High School Kagdana, Sirsa

Established in 1968 under the Department of School Education, Haryana, Govt. Girls High School Sirsa has been a cornerstone of girls' education in the Sirsa district for over five decades.

Our school has consistently maintained high academic standards, producing students who have excelled in state and national examinations. We are committed to providing quality education to girls from all socioeconomic backgrounds.

With a dedicated team of qualified teachers, well-equipped laboratories, a library, and sports facilities, we provide a holistic learning environment that prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow.

  • Established 1968
  • Affiliation Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE)
  • School Type Government Girls High School
  • Classes 6th to 10th Standard
  • Medium Hindi & English
  • Managed by Dept. of School Education, Haryana
