🏫 School Overview

Govt. Girls High School Kagdana is a government-run institution located in Village Kagdana, Tehsil Nathusari Chopta, District Sirsa, Haryana (Pin – 125110). Operating under the Haryana School Education Department, the school exclusively serves girls from Class VI to Class X.


The school is affiliated with the Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE), Bhiwani, and follows the Haryana Government curriculum with Hindi and English as the mediums of instruction.

🎯 Vision

To be a centre of excellence in girls' education that transforms lives, builds confidence and empowers every girl to contribute to society and the nation through knowledge and values.

📌 Mission

  • Provide quality, free and compulsory education to all girls
  • Create a safe, inclusive and motivating learning environment
  • Promote holistic development — academic, cultural and physical
  • Bridge the gender education gap in rural Haryana
  • Prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century
  • Instill values of discipline, respect and national pride

School History

✦

Our journey of nearly four decades

1985
School established as Govt. Girls Middle School, Kagdana under Haryana Education Department
1993
Upgraded to High School; Class IX introduced with affiliation from HBSE, Bhiwani
1995
Class X added; first batch appeared for HBSE Board Examination
2002
New school building constructed; Library and Science Laboratory established
2010
Mid-Day Meal Programme launched; scholarship schemes for SC/BC/Minority students expanded
2017
Computer Lab established under Digital India / Smart School Programme
2020
Renovated classrooms; new separate washroom block for students constructed
2024
School website launched for digital transparency with parents and students

📋 Admission Information

Eligibility

  • Girls students only
  • Classes VI to X (age appropriate)
  • Students from village Kagdana and nearby areas
  • Must have passed the previous class
  • Priority to students from weaker economic backgrounds

Documents Required

  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Mark sheet / report card of last class
  • Aadhar Card (student & parents)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • 2 Passport-size photographs
  • Income certificate (for fee waiver)
📅 Admission Period: April to June each year. Contact school office for current year admission schedule and requirements.
