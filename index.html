Principal's Message

👩
Mr. Sunil Beniwal
Principal, GGHS Kagdana Sirsa
"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Welcome to Govt. Girls High School Kagdana, Sirsa. Since our establishment in 1968, we have been committed to providing quality education to girls from all walks of life in the Sirsa district.

Our school stands as a beacon of hope and opportunity. We believe every student carries within herself the potential for greatness, and our role is to nurture that spark through rigorous academics, co-curricular activities, and values-based education.

I invite parents and students to make the most of the resources and opportunities available here. Together, we will continue to write stories of success and achievement.
