About Our School

Govt. Girls High School Kagdana is a government institution under the Haryana Education Department, serving girls of Village Kagdana and surrounding areas. Offering classes from VI to X, the school follows the HBSE curriculum.

For nearly four decades, GGHS Kagdana has been a pillar of girls' education in the region, committed to academic excellence, character development and holistic growth.

🎯 Vision

Every girl educated, empowered and inspired to lead.

📌 Mission

Providing quality, inclusive education to all girls.

🌟 Excellence

Consistent top results in HBSE examinations.

🤝 Community

Serving village Kagdana and Nathusari Chopta block.
